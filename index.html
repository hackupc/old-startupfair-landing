Startup Fair 2016

STARTUP FAIR is the first career fair for startups held at UPC.
We bring together the brightest students and high potential startups to foster networking experiences.
Join us on May 19th at UPC Campus

Connecting the best developers and computer science students with the most attractive startups

UPC Founders Lab and Hackers@UPC have prepared this awesome event, at informatics faculty of the Polytechnic University of Catalonia (UPC)

  • Location

Startups

The most innovative and irruptive startups from Barcelona will be recruiting talented and passionate technical students from UPC. Attending the Start-up Fair UPC is an excellent way to get in touch with the best computer science students and developers.

Pitch Session

The startups will have the chance to pitch during 5 minutes to the computer science students explaining the reason why the startup is performing so well and what the career plans are for the tremendously motivated youth.

Virtual Reality

We will host some Virtual Reality companies so that students will be able to try out the newest VR Products. This will represent the first event actively dedicated to Virtual Reality in Barcelona. This VR event will be placed along with the startups, attracting the top computer science students to the fair.
